OUR MISSION

SHE IS AI exists to champion women and diverse voices in shaping the future of technology through creativity, leadership, and innovation.

Inclusion First

Prioritize ethical representation and equity in everything we do.

Economic Justice

Equitable access to income, ownership, career growth, and fair value exchange across every industry.

Triple Bottom Line

Elevate member authority, protect the planet, and create profit powering pathways.

Empowered Education

We upskill, elevate, and amplify representation of women in AI.

Ethical Innovation

Champion responsible and human-centered AI development.

Creativity & Collaboration

Improve AI with unbiased creative depth, culture and lived experience.
