OUR MISSION

At SHE IS AI, we champion women and diverse voices shaping the future of technology through creativity, leadership, and innovation.

We believe that inclusion, ethics, and imagination belong at the center of AI, not on the sidelines. Every story we tell, every class we teach, and every opportunity we create builds a more equitable, intelligent, and human-centered future.

Inclusion First

We commit to ethical representation, cultural humility, and global equity in everything we do.

Economic Justice

We advance equitable access to income, ownership, and opportunity, ensuring women and underrepresented innovators benefit from the value they create.

Triple Bottom Line

We elevate member authority, protect the planet, and design profitable pathways that sustain people and purpose.

Empowered Education

We upskill, amplify, and open doors for women in AI, creating access to knowledge, leadership, and generational change.

Ethical Innovation

We champion AI that is responsible, transparent, and rooted in human dignity.

Creativity & Collaboration

We believe culture, creativity, and lived experience make AI smarter and the future brighter.

ETHICS & CORE PRINCIPLES

These values are not optional. They are the DNA of our global community.

Ethics

AI must serve humanity and human dignity. No exploitation, no shortcuts.

Equity

Fair access and opportunity for all; removing systemic barriers.

Inclusion

Diverse voices don't just participate. They lead and shape outcomes.

Empowerment

Building real agency, authority, and independence for women and underrepresented voices.

Creative Innovation

Honoring cultural wisdom and imaginative expression in AI.

Sustainability

Long-term impact that strengthens communities and protects our planet.

Transparency

Clear, honest communication about how decisions are made and how AI is used.

Accountability

Systems that ensure we practice what we preach, with measurable impact.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about joining SHE IS AI

What is SHE IS AI?
SHE IS AI is a global ecosystem, media platform, and business hub dedicated to artificial intelligence, creativity, and leadership. Our mission is to elevate, educate, and connect women and underrepresented voices in AI, shaping a future where technology is inclusive, ethical, and centered on human values.
Who can join SHE IS AI?
We welcome women, underrepresented voices, and supportive allies who are passionate about ethical AI. Whether you're a founder, artist, researcher, educator, or AI enthusiast, there's a place for you in our community.
How much does membership cost?
Our Skool community membership is $19 USD/month. Scholarships are available for those who need financial support. Simply email amanda@sheisai.ai to apply.
What are the benefits of becoming a Brand Ambassador?
Ambassadors receive priority speaking opportunities, features in SHE IS AI Magazine, event discounts, leadership pathways, access to the XPERT Agency, and opportunities to teach in our Academy. You'll also earn 40% affiliate income on community referrals.
Can I contribute content even if I'm not a member?
Yes! We welcome article submissions, AI art, and speaking proposals from everyone. Visit our submissions page or email sheisai@sheisai.ai to get started.
What is the XPERT Agency?
The XPERT Agency is a vetted directory of AI experts including strategists, consultants, educators, artists, and innovators. Being listed gives you access to paid projects, partnerships, and global opportunities.
How do I access the full onboarding guide?
Full access to our comprehensive onboarding materials is provided to community members after joining. This ensures our proprietary content, frameworks, and resources are shared within our trusted ecosystem.
What if I can't afford the membership fee?
We believe inclusion should never depend on financial means. Apply for our SHE IS AI Scholarship Program by emailing amanda@sheisai.ai. We want passionate contributors to join regardless of current financial circumstances.

CONFIDENTIAL CONTENT & COMMUNITY ACCESS

The comprehensive SHE IS AI Global Onboarding & Welcome Guide contains proprietary frameworks, training materials, and strategic resources developed exclusively for our community members.

Full access to these materials (including detailed ethics training, cultural competency frameworks, implementation tools, and operational guidelines) is provided upon joining our community. This ensures our intellectual property and sensitive organizational information remains within our trusted ecosystem.

By becoming a member or ambassador, you agree to maintain the confidentiality of proprietary content and not share externally without explicit permission. This protects the integrity of our work and honors the collective wisdom of our global network.
